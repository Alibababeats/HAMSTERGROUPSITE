Our Focus

Problem Statement

Young adults in Rockville, MD struggle to voice their opinions on school violence. They need to be connected with opportunities to be heard.

🎯

Problem Domain

  • Civic engagement
  • Political participation
  • Local issues
  • School violence
👥

Audience

  • Young adults
  • Concerned citizens
  • Local organizers
  • Local government
⚠️

Constraints

  • Lack of communication from local gov
  • Poor information pipeline
  • Unclear where to start
